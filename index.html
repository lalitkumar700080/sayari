💖 Pyar Ki Shayaris

Tumhare naam ke saath hi mera naam jod do, Hathon ki lakeeron se isko mod do, Zindagi bhar saath rahne ka vaada karo, Aur har ek pal mein pyar ko jod do.

Ishq ne jab bhi chaha, apne andaaz se chaha, Humne usko hamesha hi, har ek saans se chaha.

Dil mein tere liye pyar hai, Aankhon mein tera khumar hai, Tujhse milkar jannat mili, Dil ko sukoon aaya hai.

Pyar ke rang mein rang jao, Jeevan ki har khushi pao, Zindagi ko haseen banao, Apne pyar ko gale lagao.

Tere pyar mein doobne ko jee chahta hai, Har pal tera hi saath chahta hai, Ye dil hai hi aisa, Bas tujhe hi chahta hai.

Har pal tera hi intezar rahega, Is dil ko sirf tera hi pyar rahega, Jab tak saansein chalengi, Tera hi khumar rahega.

Pyar ka rishta anmol hai, Iska har pal bemisal hai, Dil se nikle har alfaaz, Bas tera hi khayal hai.

Pyar ki gehrai mein kho jao, Mohabbat ki duniya mein aao, Dil se dil ko milao, Zindagi ko rangeen banao.

Tera pyar meri takat hai, Tu meri har musibat mein himmat hai, Tujhse hi meri zindagi haseen hai, Tu hi meri har manzil hai.

Pyar ki raah mein chalna seekh lo, Har mushkil ko paar karna seekh lo, Zindagi mein khushiyan paane ke liye, Pyar ko dil mein basana seekh lo.

Tumhari muskan ne pagal bana diya, Dil ko tumhara deewana bana diya, Pyar ka jaadu aisa chala, Ki humko tumhara hi bana diya.

Ishq ne humein aisa bandha hai, Ki hum kahin aur nahi ja sakte, Bas tumhare paas hi rahna chahte hain, Aur kuch nahi chahte.

Tere pyar ka nasha hai, Har pal tera hi khayal hai, Yeh dil hai bas tumhara, Yahi iska jawab hai.

Pyar ki dor se bandhe hain, Ek doosre ke saathi ban gaye hain, Zindagi bhar saath nibhayenge, Yahi humne vaada kiya hai.

Tere bina zindagi adhoori hai, Tu hi meri har khushi hai, Pyar ka rishta jo banaya hai, Woh sabse khoobsurat hai.

Mohabbat ki aag mein jalna seekh lo, Pyar ke samandar mein tairna seekh lo, Zindagi ko haseen banane ke liye, Pyar ko dil mein basana seekh lo.

Dil mein chupa hai ek khayal, Tera hi khayal hai, Pyar ka rishta anmol hai, Bas yahi iska jawab hai.

🕊️ Zindagi Ki Shayaris

Zindagi ek safar hai, Jahan mod hain anek, Kabhi khushi, kabhi gham, Har pal hai ek naya sabak.

Apni zindagi ki kahani mein, Har mod par ek naya kirdar hai, Kabhi hum haste hain, Kabhi hum rote hain, Yahi toh zindagi ka pyar hai.

Zindagi mein har pal naya rang hai, Kabhi khushi, kabhi gham, Kabhi hansi, kabhi aansu, Yahi toh zindagi ka niyam hai.

Zindagi ek khwab hai, Jahan har khwab adhura hai, Apne sapnon ko poora karo, Yahi zindagi ka safar hai.
